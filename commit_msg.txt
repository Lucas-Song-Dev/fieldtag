Initial commit: FieldTag Android MVP

Full Kotlin/Jetpack Compose Android application for industrial field
photo documentation on P&ID diagrams.

Core features:
- Project management (create, archive, export)
- PDF P&ID import with PdfBox text extraction
- Grid-based manual tag identification: drag-select regions on the
  PDF to detect ISA 5.1 instrument tags; one tag per selection
- Multi-page diagram viewer with fit-to-canvas rendering and per-page
  instrument dot overlay (dots aligned to tag text positions)
- Per-instrument photo capture (CameraX) with role tagging:
  BEFORE / AFTER / OVERVIEW / DETAIL / NAMEPLATE / DURING / SAFETY
- Search, filter, and sort instruments by type, page, or alphabetically
- Export: PDF field report and ZIP photo archive
  ZIP structure: {Project}/{Tag}/{ROLE}/{Tag}_{date}_{id}.jpg
- Room (SQLite) persistence for projects, P&ID docs, instruments, media
